## 🎭 Scene-by-Scene Script

### 🎬 Scene 1: The Hook & Login (30s)
* **Action**: Open [vypar-ai.vercel.app](https://vypar-ai.vercel.app), enter email `nikhil@organicfarms.yesai` and password `123456`, click **Enter Business OS**.
* **What to Say**:
  > *"Good day, everyone. Today I'm showcasing **VyaparAI**—a decision-making Business Operating System for retail merchants. Unlike static billing systems, VyaparAI acts as an active assistant. I'll login as the owner of our store using secure JWT auth."*

---

### 📊 Scene 2: Dashboard & AI Health Narrative (1m)
* **Action**: Land on the Dashboard. Point to the **AI Business Health Score** card. Click the **Voice Summary** icon (if audio is enabled) or hover over it.
* **What to Say**:
  > *"On the dashboard, we track real-time revenue, profit margins, and outstanding credits. Our standout feature is the **AI Business Health Score**.*
  > *This is resolved using deterministic business rules, which are then parsed by **Gemini 3.1** to generate a voice and text brief. Rather than checking spreadsheets, the owner receives a natural language diagnostic overview instantly on start."*

---

### 🧠 Scene 3: AI Business Brain (1m)
* **Action**: Click **Ask Business Brain** in the sidebar. Click the query chip: *"I have ₹50,000. Which products should I restock?"* and click **Ask**.
* **What to Say**:
  > *"Next is the **AI Business Brain**. Instead of guessing stock orders, the merchant inputs a restock query. Gemini 3.1 analyzes current inventory velocity, average daily sales, and upcoming local festivals.*
  > *It builds a precise purchase plan, explaining exactly why to buy each item, estimating costs, and projecting margins—all optimized to fit the custom budget."*

---

### ⚡ Scene 4: Billing & Local-First Offline Sync (1.5m)
* **Action**: 
  1. Go to **Sales / Invoice** in the sidebar. Select *Prabal Talukdar*. Click a few items to add to the cart.
  2. Open DevTools (`F12`), go to **Network** and set to **Offline**.
  3. Point to the toast alert: *"Connection lost. Operating in local-first backup mode."*
  4. Toggle Interstate (IGST) or click a button to show the page is still interactive.
  5. Set the Network back to **Online**. Show the green sync toast.
* **What to Say**:
  > *"Let's checkout. The invoice builder handles dynamic GST calculations. Now, underground markets or local outages drop internet frequently. Watch what happens when I cut the connection.*
  > *The UI transitions instantly to a local-first offline state using browser IndexedDB caching. I can continue billing normally. Once the connection is restored, the offline queue syncs changes straight to MongoDB Atlas in the background."*

---

### 🎯 Scene 5: Churn Engine & Closing Pitch (30s)
* **Action**: Go to **CRM / Customers** in the sidebar. Point out the "Churn Risk" badges. Click **Analyze Churn** on a high-risk customer to show the copied discount campaign draft.
* **What to Say**:
  > *"Finally, our CRM computes a **Churn Risk Index** for each customer. For high-risk buyers, Gemini automatically drafts a custom retention discount pitch that the owner can instantly copy and text.*
  > *In summary: VyaparAI turns passive billing data into active, local-first business growth. Thank you!"*

---

## 💡 Pitching Tips
* **No Pauses**: Keep speaking during page transitions. Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite handles queries in ~1.5s, keeping the demo highly engaging.
* **Key Phrase to Emphasize**: *"This is decision-making AI, not a Q&A chatbot."* (Say this during Scene 2 or 3).
